---
name: Santa Monica Agenda Source
description: Where to find Santa Monica council meeting agendas and staff reports online
type: reference
---

Santa Monica city council agendas and staff reports are available at https://www.smgov.net/departments/clerk/agendas.aspx. Regular meetings are typically at 5:30 PM and special meetings at 5:35 PM on the same date. Both must be checked for a complete picture.

Airport conversion project public input is at smacproject.com.
